Will's Game of Chance

From 1 to 5 Players

Tired of the House winning all the time? Will's Game of Chance (from 1 to 5 Players) is truly a game of chance with no favoritism for the House. Each Player starts with a wallet of $1,000. Players place bets which are then reflected in the pot, and sumulteneously roll a pair of dice. Whoever has the highest combination of numbers wins the round.
